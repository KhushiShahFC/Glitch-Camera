KHUSHI'S GLITCH CAMERA


I created a glitch camera to emphasize the digital age and how much we were dependent on it during Covid and even now. The first camera has a rectangle that uses a blend mode to stress how glitchy zoom is. After a while the filter crashes itself which worked in my favor to explain the idea more. I also made another filter that does not crash and uses glitch pixelation images and it interactive. I used an external website's library for the images and how to set it up.
